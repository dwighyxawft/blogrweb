Designed for the Future

Introducing an Inextensible Editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos and Markdown. Extensibility with plugins and themes provide easy ways to add functionality or change the looks of a blog.

Robust Content Management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format or flow. With this functionality, you are in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With reliability and speed in mind, worldwide data centers provide the backbone for ultra-fast connectivity. This ensures your site will load instantly, no matter where the readers are, keeping your site competitive

Free, open, simple

Blogr is a free open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third party commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Analytics. The Architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn.

Powerful Tooling

Batteries Included. We build a simple and straightforward CLI tools that makes customization and deployment a breeze, but capable of producing the most complicated sites.
